The Harvard Crimson's Journalism Awards program will recognize outstanding student work from a variety of different categories. Some options include the ability to enter as an individual or as a school. All award categories will feature multiple ranks of award winners.

  • Categories
Best Overall Newspaper
Entry Fee: $35		 Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of writing, reporting, design, photography, and breadth of coverage.
Best Newspaper Design
Entry Fee: $35		 Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of professional and creative layout design.
Best Website
Entry Fee: $20		 Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of website design and infrastructure; make use of Internet-capabilities to present news in creative and innovative ways.
Reporter of the Year
Entry Fee: $35		 Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of writing and reporting and show sensitivity to issues where necessary.
Best News Article
Entry Fee: $20		 Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing and reporting and the relevancy of the issue; comprehensively inform the reader.
Best Feature/In-Depth
Entry Fee: $20		 Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing, reporting, and organization of ideas; present a multi-faceted story with nuance and clearly link ideas.
Best Sports Article
Entry Fee: $20		 Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing, analysis, and reporting.
Best Arts Reporting
Entry Fee: $20		 Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing and reporting.
Best Arts Review
Entry Fee: $20		 Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing and analysis.
Best Opinion Article
Entry Fee: $20		 Demonstrate outstanding quality of writing; make a clear, skillful, and reasonable argument.
Best Opinion Columnist
Entry Fee: $20		 Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of writing over multiple pieces in a single column; make clear, skillful, and reasonable arguments.
Best Staff Editorials
Entry Fee: $20		 Consistently demonstrate clear, concise, and accessible arguments made as an organized editorial board; submissions that relate to school and local issues and do so effectively will be especially strong.
Best News Photographer
Entry Fee: $20		 Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of photography and image quality; supplement and complement accompanying news articles.
Best Sports Photographer
Entry Fee: $20		 Consistently demonstrate outstanding quality of photography and image quality; supplement and complement accompanying sports articles.
Best Illustration
Entry Fee: $20		 Demonstrate outstanding artistic quality; clearly present information or an opinion.
Best Informational Graphic
Entry Fee: $20		 Demonstrate outstanding design quality; comprehensively and clearly communicate information.
