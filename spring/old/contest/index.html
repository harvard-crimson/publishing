Crimson Journalism Contest

The Spring Journalism features an annual Journalism contest in which students from attending schools may submit their works in the following categories:

  • Best News Article
  • Best Feature Article
  • Best Sports Article
  • Best Editorial Writing
  • Best Design
  • Best Website
  • Best Photo
  • Best Art Review
  • Overall Excellence in Journalism
